About Us

Your Trusted Cruise Partner in Gokarna

Namaste Gokarna Cruise brings you the most refreshing ocean journey from Tadadi Port. We offer premium sightseeing cruises covering 5 stunning beaches, dolphin watching, river–sea sangam point, and thrilling adventure activities including kayaking, snorkeling (water clarity dependent), and swimming.

Dolphin Sightseeing

Spot beautiful dolphins in their natural habitat along the Gokarna coast.

Beach Hopping

Visit famous beaches such as Paradise, Half-Moon, Om Beach & more.

Adventure Activities

Enjoy kayaking, swimming, snorkeling and fun beachside activities.

Experienced Crew

Our skilled and friendly team ensures a safe and memorable experience.
